Epicurus

From the teachings of Epicurus, the ancient Greek philosopher.

This quote represents the core of Epicurean philosophy, focusing on the pursuit of happiness through simple pleasures and the avoidance of pain. Epicurus believed that the ultimate goal of life is to achieve tranquility and freedom from fear, and that pleasure is the natural goal of life. This means that instead of seeking excessive wealth or fame, we should focus on attaining personal peace and contentment by understanding our desires and moderating them.
