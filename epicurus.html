Shapeless

From *Discourses and Selected Writings* by Epictetus.

This quote from the ancient Greek philosopher Epictetus speaks about how we allow the value of external things to dominate our souls. As human beings, we already naturally flow like water, with everything we need to be happy at our disposal. The issue is that society places too much value on things outside ourselves, often without questioning why we value them. Though it may be obscured by daily, greedy thoughts, our inner nature remains unchanged and ready to flow like water.
